today i found out what my biggest fear was.

i never knew how to answer that question but i think i found out today.

my biggest fear is to grow bitter.

become a sad angry man.

blaming everything and the sky while living out their best days picking imaginary fights at people in their head.

whoever hurt me are scared or angry or both.

as i am.

i don't want to further enable that misery. i don't want to go to war.

the people who wronged me, i'll keep loving them as i used to.

i'll love them and no one has to know.

i'll get angry as long as it's the kind that fades after a dozen breaths. nothing lasts forever.

i'll not let that build up like sediment in a cave.

i only want to love and claw my way out to keep loving, and growing, and searching.

i'm fine with burning like these catastrophic wildfires.

i'm looking forward to it. i'm not scared of burning i'm only scared of misgrowing.
